# Example: Market Research

Prompt:

```text
帮我调研一下 AI 代码工具市场，开源工具和方法论都要看。
```

Expected route:

```text
deep-research
-> Gate / Clarify
-> Wide Evidence And Visible Brainstorm
-> Adaptive Grillme
-> Narrowed Evidence Pass
-> decision_brief or PRD readiness
```

Expected behavior:

- Ask or infer target user, pain, and success criteria.
- Cover both open-source/tools and methodology/frameworks.
- Include competitors/products and user pain if the research is substantial.
- Show 3-5 directions before choosing one.
- Do not write a PRD unless the user wants a product plan and readiness is met.

Good first answer shape:

```text
Current stage: Gate / Clarify
Goal: decide what kind of market research this should become.
Done when: target reader, decision purpose, and success standard are clear enough.

One strong question: who is this research for: a founder choosing a product direction, a buyer choosing tools, an investor judging the market, or a student learning the space?
```
